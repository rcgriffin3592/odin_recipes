Fried Plantains

Description

If Congri is the classic savory Cuban side dish, fried plantains are it's classic, but sweet, counterpart.

This incredibly versatile fruit pairs well with almost every Latin dish imaginable and is incredibly easy to prepare. Requiring just a pan, some plantains, and a little oil this side will quickly become a staple when you're in need of something quick and easy to throw on the plate. They're pretty good by themselves as well!

Ingredients

  • 1 quart oil for frying
  • 2 plantains

Steps

  1. Preheat oil in a large, deep skillet over medium high heat.
  2. Peel the plantains and cut them in half. Slice the halves lengthwise into thin pieces.
  3. Fry the pieces until browned and tender. Drain excess oil on paper towel.
